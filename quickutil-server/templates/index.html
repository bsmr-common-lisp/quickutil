What's Quickutil?

Quickutil is an application and library for distributing Common Lisp utilities, aiming to solve the utility problem—everyone has their own utility library and de facto standard ones are too slowly moving. Quickutil is nimble, and only includes what you need.

It is similar in spirit to Quicklisp, in that utilities can automatically be downloaded and installed to projects.

  • 1. Installation

                (ql:quickload :quickutil-client)
  • Find utilities

    2. Load a Utility

                > (qtl:quickload :iota)
    
            > (qtl:quickload :riffle)

  • 3. Use a Utility

                > (qtl:riffle (qtl:iota 5) '--)
